What we check for every export

The app walks you through the same DS-160–style requirements as a live checklist after each successful crop.

  • Print size — 2 × 2 in (51 × 51 mm), square crop.
  • Digital resolution — square side between 600 and 1200 px; the app shows your exact export size (e.g. 1200 × 1200).
  • Eye position — from the bottom of the frame, U.S. guidance targets 56–69%; we display your measured percentage.
  • Head height — 50–69% of the image height; we show your measured value against the template.
  • Background — plain white or off-white after processing.
  • Lighting — even lighting; avoid heavy shadows on the face (follow our photo tips when shooting).
  • File format — exported as JPEG for upload or printing.
  • No biometric editing — we do not reshape or slim the outline of the head, face, or neck; cropping and background replacement only.

Final acceptance is always at the discretion of the U.S. embassy, consulate, or the system you upload to. Use official State Department instructions when in doubt.
