Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

The short version

US Visa Photo processes everything on your iPhone. We never see your photos. We don't collect personal data, we don't use analytics, and we don't run ads. The only network activity is Apple's own in-app purchase system.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No photos leave your device. All image processing — face detection, cropping, background removal — happens entirely on your iPhone using Apple's on-device Vision framework. No images are uploaded to any server.
  • No analytics or tracking. We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or advertising frameworks.
  • No accounts required. You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use this app.
  • No network requests. The app does not make any network connections for its core functionality.

Photos & Biometric Data

The app requests access to your photo library solely to let you select a photo for processing. The selected photo is processed entirely in memory on your device and is never transmitted externally.

While the app uses Apple's Vision framework to detect facial landmarks (face position, eye location, head boundaries), no biometric data is stored, exported, or shared. These measurements exist only in temporary memory during processing and are discarded when you close the app.

In-App Purchases

Payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any transaction details beyond what Apple provides to developers (anonymous transaction records). Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles payment data.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no advertising networks, analytics platforms, or external data processors of any kind.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data from any user, this applies universally.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Since we have no way to contact users (we collect no emails or identifiers), we encourage you to review this page periodically.
